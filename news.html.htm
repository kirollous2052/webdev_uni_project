Ferrari's Evolution and Global Market Share Enhancement:

April 23,2024

Under the leadership of CEO Benedetto Vigna, Ferrari is witnessing significant evolution and enhancement of its global market share.

Since taking the helm in 2021, Vigna has doubled the company's value, with the share price soaring to $389. He achieved this through reducing bureaucracy and fostering closer relationships with employees, which contributed to faster decision-making and product improvements. Ferrari is also moving away from traditional engines, with hybrid cars making up half of the company's sales last year. This development reflects Ferrari's commitment to innovation and excellence in the luxury car industry.

Ferrari and Electric Vehicles:

February 2,2024

Ferrari plans to launch its first fully electric car in 2025, noting that hybrid vehicles accounted for half of the company's sales last year.

This shift reflects Ferrari's response to global changes and a move towards sustainability and innovation in the luxury car market. The company is developing new technologies to enhance the performance of electric cars and ensure a unique driving experience that aligns with Ferrari's reputation.

Ferrari's Profit Expectations for 2023:

january 16,2024

Ferrari expects 2023 to be better than the previous year, with net revenues projected to reach approximately 5.7 billion euros.

The company ended the last year with strong financial results, exceeding expectations and setting new records. These forecasts reflect the company's continuous growth and ability to adapt to global economic challenges.

Ferrari and Autonomous Driving Technology:

march 20,2024

Despite the global trend towards autonomous driving, Ferrari maintains its stance against surrendering to this technology. Instead,

the company focuses on developing its first electric vehicles, planning to launch them by 2025. Ferrari believes that driving should remain an interactive and personal experience and is working to integrate modern technology with classic design to preserve the essence of the brand.
